163 Sterling Road

17' Ceilings, skylights and ground floor access.

 

163 Sterling Rd. features modern remodelling of industrial spaces. Large open rooms allow for individual customization to meet any business's creative needs. 

Some units feature patios with distant views of the lower west end.

 
 

The Neighbourhood.

 

The building sits comfortably in the neighbourhood of the Lower Junction Triangle a large-scale, fast growing community.

Located right across the street is the historic Tower Automotive Building, the soon to be home of the Museum of Contemporary Canadian Art. Nearby you can also find the Nestlé’s chocolate factory, the Henderson Brewery, two train lines, and 2 streetcar routes. This up and coming neighbourhood has seen a surge of new employment activity in the creative fields and moving forward promises an exciting evolution.

 
 
 
 

The Lower Junction promises to be a hub of activity in the years to come.
