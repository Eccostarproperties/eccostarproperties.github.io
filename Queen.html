909-911 queen st EAST

Historic frontage, 12' ceilings, skylights, exposed brick, and hardwood floors.

 

909-911 Queen Street East features all the characteristics of trendy modern spaces. Plenty of natural light and open space makes the building conducive to creativity.

Some units feature patios with clear views of the city skyline.

 
 

The Neighbourhood.

 

Nestled in the Queen Street East neighbourhood of Leslieville, access to transportation, food, parks, culture, and more is almost unparalleled.

Known for great brunch, art and design, Leslieville offers many restaurants and shops within walking distance. A great sense of community and access to transportation makes connects the neighbourhood to the social fabric of Toronto.

 
 
 
 

Leslieville contains some of the most walkable streets in Toronto. Place yourself in the middle of it all at 909-911 Queen St East.
